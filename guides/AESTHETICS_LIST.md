# AESTHETICS LIST

## High Fashion & Avant-Garde

### 1. Warcore / Techwear

- **Color:** Monochrome matte black, slate grey, olive drab, accents of safety orange.
- **Texture:** Ballistic nylon, ripstop fabric, waterproof Gore-Tex, heavy buckles, rubberized matte surfaces.
- **Camera/Optics:** 85mm lens for compression; sharp focus on gear details.
- **Lighting:** Harsh, overhead fluorescent or cold white street lighting (4000K-5000K) to emphasize synthetic materials.
- **Shot Angle:** Low angle (wormâ€™s eye view) to impose dominance; threatening posture.

### 2. Avant-Garde Goth

- **Color:** Vantablack (light-absorbing), deep charcoal, pale skin tones, crimson accents.
- **Texture:** Distressed leather, heavy wool, latex, layered sheer fabrics like tulle or organza, untreated metal.
- **Camera/Optics:** 35mm film stock with slight grain (e.g., Kodak Tri-X 400); high contrast.
- **Lighting:** Rembrandt lighting (strong chiaroscuro); single light source creating deep shadows on the face.
- **Shot Angle:** Dutch angle (tilted) to create unease and disorientation.

### 3. Deconstructivism

- **Color:** Off-whites, raw canvas beige, stark black stitching, muted industrial greys.
- **Texture:** Frayed edges, exposed seams, inside-out fabric, raw concrete backgrounds, jagged silhouettes.
- **Camera/Optics:** Macro lens (100mm) for texture detail; shallow depth of field (f/2.8) blurring the background chaos.
- **Lighting:** Diffused but directional skylight; cool tones to highlight texture without washing it out.
- **Shot Angle:** Close-up or medium shot, focusing on the "broken" or asymmetrical elements of the subject.

### 4. Chrome & Liquid Metal

- **Color:** Silver, mercury, reflected environment colors (neon blues/pinks), high-gloss black.
- **Texture:** Perfectly smooth, reflective, viscous fluid, hard polished steel, distortion on surfaces.
- **Camera/Optics:** Wide-angle lens (24mm) to exaggerate reflections; polarization filter to manage glare.
- **Lighting:** Multiple point-source lights or HDR environment maps to create complex specular highlights.
- **Shot Angle:** Extreme close-up on faces or objects, distorted by the curvature of the reflective material.

### 5. Cyber-Trash / Scavenger Chic

- **Color:** Mismatched plastics (faded beige, cyan), rust orange, copper wire, dirty white.
- **Texture:** Tangled wires, duct tape, scratched plastic, circuit boards, grease, grime.
- **Camera/Optics:** "Disposable camera" aesthetic; plastic lens soft focus, flash washout, vignetting.
- **Lighting:** Direct on-camera flash (harsh and flat) causing overexposure on shiny plastic parts.
- **Shot Angle:** Eye-level, candid snapshot style; slightly off-center framing.

## Digital & Glitch

### 6. Glitchcore

- **Color:** RGB split (red/green/blue offset), neon magenta, cyan, static noise greys.
- **Texture:** Pixelation, jpeg artifacts, screen tearing lines, databending (smearing).
- **Camera/Optics:** Digital sensor clipping; simulated sensor damage; chromatic aberration.
- **Lighting:** Strobe lighting; high-speed sync freezing motion but with digital tearing.
- **Shot Angle:** Frontal, confrontational; subject often looks directly into the lens.

### 7. Datamosh

- **Color:** Bleeding colors, muddy transitions between frames, high saturation blocks.
- **Texture:** Compression artifacts, "macroblocking" (large squares), fluid/melting pixels.
- **Camera/Optics:** Simulated low bitrate video compression; motion blur baked into the image.
- **Lighting:** Shifted hues; lighting that appears to "lag" behind the subject.
- **Shot Angle:** Dynamic motion shots where the movement causes the visual breakdown.

### 8. Acid Graphics

- **Color:** High-vis yellow, lime green, hot pink against absolute #000000 black.
- **Texture:** Vector-sharp lines, dithering patterns (checkerboard shading), liquid chrome typography.
- **Camera/Optics:** No lens distortion (orthographic feel); ultra-sharp digital rendering.
- **Lighting:** None (flat shading) or extreme rim lighting that creates glowing outlines.
- **Shot Angle:** Graphic design composition; centered, symmetrical, or creating a specific shape (e.g., a skull).

### 9. Analog Horror

- **Color:** Desaturated NTSC color palette (washed out reds and blues), sepia or green tint.
- **Texture:** VHS tape noise, scanlines, magnetic distortion warping the bottom of the frame, film grain.
- **Camera/Optics:** Low-fidelity camcorder lens; poor dynamic range (blown-out highlights, crushed blacks).
- **Lighting:** Flashlight beam in a dark room (tunnel vision effect); creepy eyes reflecting light.
- **Shot Angle:** Point-of-view (POV) handheld; shaky camera movement implied.

### 10. Thermal / Infrared

- **Color:** False color spectrum: White/Red (hot), Blue/Purple (cold), or Green/Black (night vision).
- **Texture:** Grainy sensor noise, lack of surface texture (skin looks smooth/glowing), halo artifacts.
- **Camera/Optics:** FLIR (Forward-Looking Infrared) simulation; low resolution upscaled.
- **Lighting:** Emissive lighting (objects glow from within based on heat); ambient temperature contrast.
- **Shot Angle:** High-angle surveillance drone view or sniper scope POV.

## Dystopian & Industrial

### 11. Neo-Brutalism

- **Color:** Concrete grey, stale white, shadows are pitch black.
- **Texture:** Rough-cast concrete, exposed aggregate, imposing geometric slabs, minimal glass.
- **Camera/Optics:** Tilt-shift lens (to correct verticals or make structures look miniature/toy-like); deep depth of field (f/16).
- **Lighting:** Harsh midday sun creating hard, angular shadows; or heavy overcast (flat, oppressive light).
- **Shot Angle:** Extreme low angle looking up at towering structures to dwarf the viewer.

### 12. Biomechanical

- **Color:** Monochromatic metallic blues, greys, with organic fleshy pinks or translucent ambers.
- **Texture:** Ribbed piping, wet slime, chitinous exoskeletons, fused metal and bone.
- **Camera/Optics:** Macro photography; sharp details on wet surfaces; depth cuing with fog.
- **Lighting:** Rim lighting to highlight slime/wetness; volumetric fog shafts (God rays).
- **Shot Angle:** Intimate, claustrophobic framing; internal body-horror perspectives.

### 13. Dieselpunk

- **Color:** Sepia, oil black, rust red, brass, soot grey.
- **Texture:** Oily metal, rivets, smoke, leather, grease stains, heavy iron.
- **Camera/Optics:** Vintage lens emulation (Petzval) with swirling bokeh; desaturated Technicolor look.
- **Lighting:** Tungsten warmth mixed with fire/sparks; hazy atmosphere from exhaust.
- **Shot Angle:** Wide shots showing scale of machinery vs. humans; industrial landscape.

### 14. Grimdark

- **Color:** Muddy browns, olive greens, bruised purples, grey skies.
- **Texture:** Mud, rain-slicked surfaces, blood, rust, battle damage, grime.
- **Camera/Optics:** High ISO noise (grainy); 50mm lens; muted contrast (grey blacks).
- **Lighting:** Low key; practical light sources only (torches, burning fires, distant explosions).
- **Shot Angle:** Eye-level combat perspective; chaotic framing.

### 15. Post-Soviet Decay

- **Color:** Pale sky blue, grey concrete, overgrown green, faded pastel mural colors.
- **Texture:** Peeling paint, cracked pavement, dry winter grass, rusting playground equipment.
- **Camera/Optics:** Wide angle (28mm) to capture vast, empty spaces; flat focus.
- **Lighting:** Overcast, diffused soft light (shadowless); creating a feeling of coldness and isolation.
- **Shot Angle:** Static, wide landscape shots; centered horizons; emphasis on emptiness.

## Artistic & Stylized

### 16. Trash Polka

- **Color:** Strictly Black, White, and Blood Red.
- **Texture:** Ink splatters, halftone dots, brush strokes, skin texture, stencil spray paint.
- **Camera/Optics:** High-contrast graphic style; composite image feel; sharp focus.
- **Lighting:** Flat white background or skin tone; lighting is illustrative rather than realistic.
- **Shot Angle:** Collage composition; layering of elements without traditional perspective.

### 17. Chiaroscuro / Film Noir

- **Color:** High-contrast Black and White (silvery highlights).
- **Texture:** Cigarette smoke, rain on glass, silk, velvet, wet pavement.
- **Camera/Optics:** 50mm prime lens; soft focus filters (diffusion) on highlights.
- **Lighting:** Hard "venetian blind" shadows; silhouette lighting; catchlights in eyes.
- **Shot Angle:** Dutch tilts; framing through objects (doorframes, staircases).

### 18. Constructivism

- **Color:** Red, Black, White, Cream.
- **Texture:** Paper grain, matte ink, cut-out edges, bold typography blockiness.
- **Camera/Optics:** Orthographic projection (no depth perspective); geometric composition.
- **Lighting:** None (flat graphic style); purely shape and color based.
- **Shot Angle:** Dynamic diagonals; aggressive upward pointing lines.

### 19. Gothic Futurism

- **Color:** Midnight blue, gold, holographic accents, dark stone grey.
- **Texture:** Nano-carbon weave mixed with ornate stone carving; glowing circuitry, velvet.
- **Camera/Optics:** Anamorphic lens (cinematic aspect ratio); horizontal lens flares.
- **Lighting:** Bioluminescence; cool blue ambient light with warm gold practical lights.
- **Shot Angle:** Epic wide shots showing architecture; symmetry (nave of a cathedral).

### 20. Solarized

- **Color:** Inverted tones; shadows become highlights; metallic cyans, solar oranges.
- **Texture:** Metallic sheen on skin; halo effects around edges; flat mid-tones.
- **Camera/Optics:** Sabatier effect simulation; high contrast curves.
- **Lighting:** Frontal flash that is then tone-inverted; creating a "ghostly" metallic look.
- **Shot Angle:** Portrait framing; focusing on facial topography which becomes alien-like.

## Bio-Accelerationist & Transhumanist

### 21. Wetware / Bio-Circuitry

- **Concept:** The raw, messy integration of computer hardware into living tissue. Not clean cybernetics, but surgical and visceral.
- **Color:** Arterial red, bruised purple, surgical steel grey, pale flesh tones.
- **Texture:** Wet mucus, exposed muscle fibers, sleek copper wiring, silicone, condensation.
- **Camera/Optics:** Macro lens (100mm); extremely shallow depth of field focusing on the point of insertion.
- **Lighting:** Cold, clinical operating theatre lights (6000K) mixed with red warning strobes.
- **Shot Angle:** Extreme close-up (ECU) on eyes, ports, or open wounds; uncomfortable intimacy.

### 22. Osteo-Futurism / Bone-Tech

- **Concept:** Technology grown from calcium and marrow; exoskeletons that look like 3D-printed bone rather than metal.
- **Color:** Ivory white, marrow red, matte black carbon fiber, beige.
- **Texture:** Porous bone, smooth enamel, Voronoi patterns (organic web-like holes), rigid spinal structures.
- **Camera/Optics:** Portrait lens (85mm); sharp focus to highlight the structural intricacies.
- **Lighting:** Soft, diffused white light (museum lighting) casting complex shadows through the bone lattice.
- **Shot Angle:** Low angle bust shot, emphasizing the majesty and alien nature of the evolution.

### 23. Toxic Accelerationism

- **Concept:** Nature mutating rapidly due to chemical waste; aggressive, vibrant, and deadly flora taking over ruins.
- **Color:** Radioactive lime green, bioluminescent violet, asphalt black, warning yellow.
- **Texture:** Slime, thorns, pulsating veins in leaves, oily water, moss on concrete.
- **Camera/Optics:** Wide-angle (24mm) with high saturation settings; slight motion blur to suggest growth/movement.
- **Lighting:** UV / Blacklight aesthetics; objects glowing in the dark against a pitch-black background.
- **Shot Angle:** Eye-level, immersed in the overgrowth; claustrophobic framing.

### 24. Post-Human Porcelain

- **Concept:** The "uncanny valley" cyborg; faces that look like perfect, breakable dolls with no pores, hiding machinery underneath.
- **Color:** Alabaster white, gold filigree, electric blue (eyes), deep darkness.
- **Texture:** Flawless glazed ceramic skin, gold cracks (Kintsugi style), glass, robotic joints.
- **Camera/Optics:** Soft focus / "Dream" filter (Pro-Mist); slightly blooming highlights.
- **Lighting:** Halo lighting (rim light) creating a saintly or deity-like aura around the subject.
- **Shot Angle:** Symmetrical, passport-style front facing; staring directly into the soul of the viewer.

### 25. Ferrofluid Dynamics

- **Concept:** Liquid technology that is spikey, black, and magnetic; aggressive, shapeshifting symbiotes.
- **Color:** Jet black, iridescent oil slick (purple/green sheen), chrome reflections.
- **Texture:** Spikes, liquid ripples, smooth oily surfaces, sharp jagged edges.
- **Camera/Optics:** High shutter speed to freeze liquid motion; high contrast B&W rendering.
- **Lighting:** Specular highlights are key; multiple point lights to catch the tips of the liquid spikes.
- **Shot Angle:** Dynamic action shots; the subject is being consumed or enveloped by the fluid.

## Futuristic Apocalypse & Dystopia

### 26. Xeno-Crystalline

- **Concept:** A viral infection that turns flesh into crystal or glass; beautiful but fatal transmutation.
- **Color:** Translucent teal, rose quartz, jagged grey rock, pale skin.
- **Texture:** Sharp geometric facets, raw geodes, skin stretching over crystals, refraction.
- **Camera/Optics:** Prism filter effects; light refracting into rainbows; sharp focus on crystals.
- **Lighting:** Backlighting is essential to make the crystals glow and show transparency.
- **Shot Angle:** Detail shots of hands or faces beginning to crystallize.

### 27. Void-Stalker / Vantablack Tech

- **Concept:** Stealth technology so advanced it absorbs all light; silhouettes moving through a neon world.
- **Color:** #000000 (True Black), neon cyan/magenta outlines, grey fog.
- **Texture:** Matte, non-reflective, void-like negative space, hard edges against fog.
- **Camera/Optics:** Telephoto compression; high contrast curves (crushed blacks).
- **Lighting:** Silhouette lighting; background is bright (fog/neon), subject is a black hole.
- **Shot Angle:** Wide shots emphasizing the environment, with the subject as a dark, ominous presence.

### 28. Cyber-Parasitism

- **Concept:** The machine is a parasite feeding on the human host; heavy cables, life-support systems that look painful.
- **Color:** Rust orange, bruised blue, dirty white, industrial grey.
- **Texture:** Corrugated tubes, duct tape, sweat, distressed skin, rusted clamps.
- **Camera/Optics:** Gritty, documentary style; "dirty" lens with dust specks.
- **Lighting:** Flickering fluorescent tubes (green tint); uneasy, unstable lighting.
- **Shot Angle:** High angle looking down (powerless subject); or over-the-shoulder showing the parasite attached to the spine.

### 29. Nuclear Ash / Fallout

- **Concept:** The moments after the end; a world drained of color, covered in grey snow (ash).
- **Color:** Monochromatic grey scale, muted brown, faint ember orange.
- **Texture:** Particulate matter in air, thick dust, torn rags, matte gas mask rubber.
- **Camera/Optics:** Low contrast (flat); high grain/noise; focus is soft due to "haze."
- **Lighting:** Flat, diffused, skylight blocked by heavy smoke clouds; no direct sun.
- **Shot Angle:** Wide, lonely landscapes with a solitary figure; vast negative space.

### 30. Glitch-Flesh / Reality Decay

- **Concept:** The simulation is breaking; the human body is rendering incorrectly, dissolving into pixels or geometry.
- **Color:** Skin tone transitioning into RGB noise; black void; bright error colors.
- **Texture:** Low-poly geometry, pixelation, wireframe mesh, dissolving sand.
- **Camera/Optics:** Digital distortion effects; data-moshing; perspective errors.
- **Lighting:** Lighting that doesn't match the environment (e.g., subject lit from below, world lit from above).
- **Shot Angle:** Disjointed framing; limbs or heads floating or disconnected from the body.
